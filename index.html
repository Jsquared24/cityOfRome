The Macquarie University TeX Users' group is for all students, staff, researchers, and interested folk wanting to learn, enjoy, and help others with publishing documents. We help people at MQ publish professional documents with Markdown, LaTeX, Overleaf, ConTeXt, and Pandoc. Everyone is welcome, from people with no prior knowledge to experts who have already typeset a few books. Our meeting agenda is: 1. Showing off our recent TeX awesomeness. 2. A practical demonstration of TeX 3. Help, brainstorming, advice, and troubleshooting of TeX Our next meeting will be at: 11 AM on 22 June, Australian Hearing Hub, Level 2, Room 2.605.
